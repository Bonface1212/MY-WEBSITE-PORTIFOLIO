Timeless Shots, Seamless Tech...

Welcome! I'm Bonface Mamboleo, a Nairobi-based photographer, videographer, web developer, and software developer.

I specialize in capturing authentic moments—from family portraits and street photography to corporate headshots, live streaming, and events coverage. While I love using natural light, I also work with studio lighting, softboxes, LED panels, and creative lighting techniques to achieve the perfect mood and atmosphere for every shot.

Beyond photography, I craft elegant websites and dynamic applications, blending creativity with technology to bring ideas to life. Whether behind the lens or at the keyboard, my goal is to create visuals and experiences that are both beautiful and meaningful.

Let’s create something extraordinary together. ✨
