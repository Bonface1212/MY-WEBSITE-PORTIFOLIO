About Me

Hey there, I'm Bonface Mamboleo! Based in the vibrant city of Nairobi, Kenya, I am a passionate web designer, photographer, videographer, and software developer. My work revolves around blending creativity with technology to craft stunning visuals and user-friendly digital experiences.

On a personal note, I am fluent in English and Swahili, and I enjoy connecting with people from diverse backgrounds. My love for storytelling drives me to capture authentic moments through photography and videography, whether it's a family portrait, a corporate event, or a creative project.

Over the years, I have honed my skills in web development, graphic design, and multimedia production. I specialize in creating elegant websites, dynamic applications, and visually compelling content that resonates with audiences. My goal is to bring ideas to life and deliver results that exceed expectations.

When I'm not working, you can find me exploring the streets of Nairobi, capturing candid moments, or experimenting with new design trends and technologies. I also enjoy collaborating with like-minded individuals and learning from different perspectives to grow both personally and professionally.

Fun fact: I am a huge fan of natural light photography and creative lighting techniques, which I use to set the perfect mood for every shot. Whether behind the lens or at the keyboard, I am dedicated to creating visuals and experiences that are both beautiful and meaningful.

Feel free to explore my portfolio, download my CV, or reach out for collaborations. Let’s create something extraordinary together!
